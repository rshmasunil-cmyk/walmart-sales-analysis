<h1>Project Summary</h1>

End-to-end data analytics project analyzing Walmart customer sales data to uncover revenue trends, customer behavior, repeat purchase patterns, and discount effectiveness using Power BI dashboards.
This project demonstrates skills in data visualization, business analysis, KPI reporting, and insight-driven decision making.

<h2>Tools & Skills Used</h2>

<ul>
  <li>Power BI (DAX, interactive dashboards, KPI cards, slicers)</li>
  <li>Data Analysis & Visualization</li>
  <li>Business Intelligence (BI) Reporting</li>
  <li>Customer Segmentation</li>
  <li>Sales & Revenue Analysis</li>
</ul>

<h2>Dashboard Pages & Analysis</h2>

<h3>1. Sales & Revenue Overview</h3>
<h5>Visuals:</h5>

<ul>
  <li>Category-wise Sales</li>
  <li>Product-wise Sales</li>
  <li>Sales Trend by Year and Month</li>
</ul>

<h5>Insights:</h5>
<ul>
  <li>Electronics, Home, Beauty, and Clothing show balanced revenue contribution</li>
  <li>Sales peak between April 2024 and October 2024</li>
  <li>Sharp revenue decline observed in January 2025, indicating seasonality</li>
</ul>

<h3>2. Customer Demographics Analysis</h3>
<h5>Visuals:</h5>

<ul>
<li>Gender Distribution</li>
<li>Age Distribution</li>
<li>Top 10 Cities by Customer Count</li>
</ul>

<h5>Insights:</h5>
<ul>
  <li>Customer base is evenly distributed across genders</li>
  <li>Age groups 26–35 and 36–45 drive the highest purchase amounts</li>
  <i>Certain cities consistently contribute higher customer volume</i>
</ul>

<h3>3. Repeat Customer & Discount Analysis</h3>
<h5>Visuals:</h5>

<ul>
  <li>Repeat Customer Rate by Month and Year</li>
  <li>Repeated Customers by Category with Discount vs No Discount</li>
</ul>

<h5>Insights:</h5>
<ul>
  <li>Discounts have no significant impact on repeat customer rate</li>
  <li>Repeat purchases occur consistently across categories</li>
  <li>Opportunity to reduce discount dependency and improve profit margins</li>
</ul>

<h3>4. Payment Method Analysis</h3>
<h5>Visuals:</h5>

<ul>
  <li>Payment Method Distribution</li>
</ul>

<h5>Insights:</h5>
<ul>
  <li>Debit Card, Credit Card, Cash on Delivery, and UPI each contribute 25%</li>
  <li>Balanced payment usage reduces operational and payment risk</li>
</ul>

<h2>Business Recommendations</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Focus marketing efforts on 26–35 and 36–45 age groups</li>
  <li>Optimize discount strategies to protect margins</li>
  <li>Prepare operations for peak sales between April–October</li>
  <li>Maintain diversified payment methods to reduce risk</li>
</ul>

<h2>Key Learnings</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Built interactive KPI-driven Power BI dashboards</li>
  <li>Translated raw data into actionable business insights</li>
  <li>Applied analytical thinking to real-world retail data</li>
  <li>Strengthened storytelling through data visualization</li>
</ul>
